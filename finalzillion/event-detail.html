Cybersecurity: The Key to the digital enterprise

Where

Departure at 09:30 from Munkedamsveien 35 in Oslo to a secret destination

When

28 October 2019 9:30 AM - 8:00 PM

Where

Free of charge. Zillion will reimburse travel expenses for chosen students arriving from outside of Oslo.

At Zillion, we want to change the consulting industry. We believe that in order to create real impact in our clients’ involving rapid and dramatic change , need deep functional expertise combined with equally deep insights into how we are perceived by and affect the people around us.

All our new employees start this process at Zillion UpFront, an intensive three-day journey where we experience how we react physically and mentally when faced with different situations and challenges. These insights are then converted into practice in our everyday life.

Get a taste of the Zillion change journey

On Thursday 17 October 2019, we invite ambitious students to join us for a taste of the UpFront journey. We want to get to know you, give you the chance to get to know us and hopefully, also help you get to know yourself even better. You can expect to be challenged, to learn more about yourself and to try out our methods – while also getting to know some of our consultants as well as fellow students.

You will get to experience how we build great teams and learn to play to each other’s strengths in order to get to great solutions faster. And you will spend the day learning more about how we do this at Zillion. How we do this is defined by who we are and what we do.
